Angelo Roque

Hi ,I am Angelo Roque. Raised in the Quezon City, I was a curious kid who discovered the world through NatGeo, Discovery Channel, Anime and Video Games. ‍
I went to STI College to study Information Technology, I used every opportunity to learn everything about computer and there I developed a strong interest in digital graphics.
While still in college, I had an opportunity to teach at a local training center on my free time and earn extra money.
My coolest moment while doing my job is to handle projects with Universal Studios(Land before time | Curious george), Disney(Penn Zero), Nickelodeon(Mysticons) and HBO(Animals), Sitting in a meeting with this creative mastermind is sometimes overwhelming. ‍
Recently I took up Web Development from Zuitt to further broaden my knowledge as a developer and designer. So yeah! I am addicted to learning new skill.
Working in fast-paced, collaborative environments, I combine motion graphics, video editing and 3D design to produce elevated, playful and bleeding edge art that will help client elevate their brand.
